Synthetica

Synthetica is a school project of mine at Grafish Lyceum Utrecht. For the project I got three weeks to make a nightracer game together with three developers including me and three artists. In Synthetica you get to drive around in a retro city at night where your goal is to drive the best time posible.

My contributions:

  • Highscore
  • Audio
  • UI
Highscore:

I made the highscore system where everytime when you finish it checks if your current driven time is faster than your best time. When you drive a new fastest time your time will get saved in a playerpref.

Below here you can see a piece of the code for the highscore:

Here a gif of what it looks like in game when you finish. In this example I didn't get a new high score so the best time doesn't change, but you do get to see your current driven time.

Audio:

I have made a audio System where you can change the volume with sliders in the UI.

UI:

For the UI I made all de buttons in the start screen, character select, controls, finish screen and the settings menu interactable and working all together. I also made the speedometer and checkpoint counter.
